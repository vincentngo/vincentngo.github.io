# Welcome to My Blog

Hello and welcome to my personal portfolio and knowledge platform! This is the first post on this blog, and I'm excited to share my journey with you.

## What to Expect

This blog will cover a variety of topics including:

- **Software Development**: Tips, tutorials, and best practices
- **Technology**: Insights on emerging tech trends
- **Personal Projects**: Deep dives into my side projects
- **Learning**: Notes and reflections on continuous learning

## Built with Modern Tech

This platform is built using:

- Next.js 16 with App Router
- TypeScript with strict mode
- Tailwind CSS v4
- MDX for content
- Static export for GitHub Pages

## Stay Connected

Feel free to explore the posts, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or feedback!
