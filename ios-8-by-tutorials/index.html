iOS 8 by Tutorials

iOS 8 has finally been released today! I was fortunate enough to get a chance to write a book tutorial with other great authors/developers to expose all the exciting new features iOS 8 has to offer.

iOS 8 by tutorials gives you step by step instructions in using the cool and hip new features that iOS 8 brings to the table.

After a couple months of work, iOS 8 by Tutorials is finally out! I am so thankful for the opportunity to work with the team at Raywenderlich to write this book! Everyone on the team is so smart and helpful and I have learned a lot! I wrote two chapters related to live rendering in Interface builder and it was awesome! If you do decide to grab a copy, I hope you all will love reading and learning about iOS 8!
